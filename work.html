Works

We're Good

We’re Good is an installation and social commentary on consumerism through the lens of technological advancements and ethics of surveillance. Found furniture serves as the centrepiece of the space, with simulated real-time footage data of its state of abandonment through the autonomy of public surveillance and decades of furniture commercials projected on the background.
